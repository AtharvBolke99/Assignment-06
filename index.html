Kolhaleshwar Shiv Temple

Kolhaleshwar Shiv Temple is a famous temple in Kolhar, Maharashtra. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and attracts many devotees throughout the year. The structure of this temple is very beautiful and looks so attractive.

Sugarcane Factory

Kolhar is known for its sugarcane production, and the local sugarcane factory plays a vital role in the economy of the region. The factory processes sugarcane into sugar and other by-products, providing employment opportunities to many locals. This is a asia's 1st sugarcane factory located in the pravaranagar(Kolhar).
